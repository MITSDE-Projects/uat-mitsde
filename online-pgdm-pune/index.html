Global Exposure

Learning That Opens a Bigger World

MIT School of Distance Education equips working professionals with the global exposure, industry perspective & practical insights needed to navigate today’s evolving business landscape with confidence.

Corporate Collaborations: KPMG & EY

MIT School of Distance Education collaborates with KPMG & EY , two of the Big Four global consulting firms, to bring industry-relevant learning into its programs.

Through this collaboration, learners gain access to industry-recognized certifications, real-world insights & practical exposure that are seamlessly integrated into their postgraduate journey enhancing both their skills & career readiness.

Global Study Tours

MIT School of Distance Education International Study Tours offer global exposure through university workshops, industry visits & cultural experiences across Dubai, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore & Europe ending with an international university certificate.

  • Duration: 5 Days
  • Outcome: International University
  • Certificate Itinerary:
  • 1 Day: University Workshop
  • 2 Days: Industry Visits
  • 2 Days: City & Cultural Tour

International Summer Internship Program (ISIP)

The International Summer Internship Program provides learners with hands-on exposure to global business practices through industry projects, professional mentorship & cross-cultural collaboration.

The program runs for 4–12 weeks & combines virtual learning with on-site international engagement.

International Relations Office (IRO)

The International Relations Office supports global academic collaboration through student exchanges, semester-abroad opportunities, faculty exchange programs & joint research initiatives.

It also facilitates pathways for MS & PhD studies abroad, supported by international partnerships & funding programs.
